Sorry that you and your friend got taken in on these. They are just
generic fakes. They are not a copy of any particular sword. They are
just sort of Japanese sword shaped, and then marketed as genuine to the
unwary. If you type in "Viking sword" on EBay, you will see lots of
modern swords that sort of look like Viking swords, but hopefully are
described as being reproductions somewhere in the description. I doubt
if any of them is a particularly good representation of the genuine
article, either.

Bob
