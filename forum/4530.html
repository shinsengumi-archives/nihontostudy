by robsongord

It seems rather atypical of a Soten work. I was rather more interesting in
the write up, particularly the fact that almost 95 per cent of Soten pieces
are Yokohama-mono. I think the collecting public in the States needs to
realize just how much poor quality Yokohama export stuff in terms of
fittings and swords is floating around. A lot of it is very flashing
looking, but no depth of workmanship or artistic level. A lot of newbies get
sucked into buying this stuff for far more than it is worth.



Gordon
