Re: inexpensive book

by davidzincavage

I just received my copy of:

Henryk Socha, Elementy broni samurajskiej - Tsuba [Elements of the Arms of
the Samurai - The Tsuba], Dom Wydawnictwo Bellona, Warsaw, 2003.


which I ordered June 18th from

www.merlin.com.pl in Poland.

The book cost 77.50 zlotys. Shipping cost 30. zlotys. Total: 107.50 zlotys.
The zloty is trading today in the 3.85 to 3.90 to the dollar range.
That comes out to something like $28, which is heck of a deal a 232 pp,
quarto, color book. The text is in Polish, of course, but all the technical
terms are the Japanese terms everyone is accustomed to. There are 100 very
nicely full page photographed tsuba, including some very nice examples.

This book site work just like Amazon, but is all in Polish. Search on
"tsuba," and you'll find the book. It is fairly easy to order. The only
tricky part is fitting US addresses into spaces designed for
Polish-formatted addresses.
