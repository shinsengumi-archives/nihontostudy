There is a reason that text could be a problem.

In general, you're looking at 180 dpi for the recommended resolution of
photographs and pictures. You could go as low as 150dpi in some cases,
but everything over 180dpi is overkill.

For text, the line is drawn around 400dpi.

If these guys are stuck on photos, you may have a 180dpi resolution
hardcoded everywhere... which means your text will look bad.

Still worthwhile to look into, but this should be kept in mind.
