Hi all,

Sorry I was a little slow in checking this out. The Minoto Taikan had a
small sized oshigata and that compared very well. Even better was Nihonto
Zuikan -- yes, if you do not have this set of books, you will never know
what you have. There were two, first gen. oshigata here and they both
matched very well, particularly the katana oshigata. So, my call -- genuine
signature of 1st. gen. Shinano no Kami Daido, circa Kanei.

Gordon
