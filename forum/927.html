Milt,
I think that Matt is correct. As long as the 1/3 of one side is not too severely damaged and everything else about the guard is good, I think it would pass, however the point level would be significantly lower than for the same guard in overall good condition. If the rust is bad and/or the guard is only ordinary quality, you might be looking at a shinteisho level paper (60-69 points). If it was a much better than average tsuba and the rust isn't too bad, a kanteisho might be possible. It depends on just what levels of quality and condition you have.
