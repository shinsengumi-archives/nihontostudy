Re: bibliography

by drjohnplissenden

Dear David

One of the best bibliographies on Japanese language titles is to be
found in the Haynes and Burawoy '100 Selected Tsuba from European
Public Collections'. A special, numbered edition of this book included
a 'complete bibliography of publications on the subject of sword
fittings from 1693 to (the date of publication in) September 1984'.
This includes almost 400 titles, listed in the language of
publication, the degree of specialisation and the type of publication.

Sadly it contains no publications after 1984, of course.

Regards, John L.
