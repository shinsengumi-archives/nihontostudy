David,

You want to do the regular cleaning you already do, and hopefully
you're using real Japanese choji oil, not one of the cheap knockoffs.
The only difference with a newly polished blade is that you want to
be cleaning it a lot more frequently than normal, at least once a
week. Polishing takes a layer off (more if not done by an expert),
and leaves the blade that much more vulnerable to humidity the first
couple of months. I don't know where you're located, but the
frequency of your cleanings would depend on the environment, too. The
more humidity the blade is exposed to, the more frequently your sword
cleaning kit should be put to use.

Hope that helps.

Andres
