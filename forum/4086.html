The look of the piece is much like Kanenori (18th century)of Aizu in
Iwashiro province who did AOI style pieces with the same metel
colour, rounded mimi and heavier feel. There is some controversy if
he was the same as Kanenori of Sendai in Rikuzen province but Haynes
feels they are different tsubako. My other thoughts had been later
Myochin or Umetada but have been ruled out.
