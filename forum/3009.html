Rich,
All that I can make out is tadashige. As you no doubt already know
there were both Joshu Umetada Tadashige and Shigemasa working in about
the right time period. The workmanship looks right for Umetada.

I don't know much about Goshu Hikone Tadashige, but at least some of
his work is in classic Soten style. I can't say that I've seen
Soten/Hikone work in the style of your tsuba.
