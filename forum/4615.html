Re: [nihontostudy] a little more direction

by musashibobenkei

To clarify a bit on the "Kunitomo" tsuba, I owned that one for a number of
years. I bought it from Skip Holbrook, and that's where the attribution came
from, I think because of the sahari inlay work reminding one of similar work
by the Kunitomo gunsmiths. No one who saw this tsuba, Jim Gilbert, Bob
Haynes, etc. could think of anything better to call it. I'm pretty sure that
Bob said he did not recognize the kao.

No papers, unless Elliot has taken that step ... it would be interesting to
see what 'box' the experts would put it in.

Craig
