Repro, Repro, Interesting (but I'm still thinking
Repro), and a Repro.

Funny interior measurements, unevenness of sukashi
along the outer circumfrence of the tsuba, and other
odd things that default me into thinking 'repros' on
all. The same sort of funny stuff appears to my eye on
all of them.
But I wonder how they were made. My feeling is
originals are used. If so, and it is simply some fault
of measuring the originals, then it follows the next
generation could be very convincing.

Maybe Peter is right and they are just very
overcleaned, but I vote 'repros'.
