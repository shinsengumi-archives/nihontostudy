chicago show...... quick report

by bungotakadasword

gang,
Just came back from the Chicago show...............
a lot of old faces were not there but the core die hard table holders
were there holding the fort.
The meibutsu exhibition , as always, is outstanding with Bob Benson
giving commentary und answer any questions put forth by the
attendees. This year's exhibition was on " Yamato blades ". Also
there was a tsuba exihition on Kyo-sukashi tsuba.

Even though the attendance was noticably lower than previous years, I
noticed a heightened level of activities among dealers,
collectors/table holders. A lot of horse tradings took place to
everyone's advantage. I personally sold quite a few tsuba und
done a few trades which I am very happy with. There's no last minutes
firehouse sales as the prices were set quite low to begin with, so if
you have cash, many good deals were possible.
A fellow listeros had a great display of his personal collection of
Christian theme tsuba................ simply OUTSTANDING !!!!!!!! he
corned the market on that theme !!!!!

Roy H. had a display of an outstanding tachi koshirae that used to
belong to gen. Sugiyama und a very nice display of HIGH QUALITY tauba
( among them a Hoan tsuba that made some people hyperventilate )....
in short, a mini Meibutsu room display right there in the room for
all to see through out the show.
If you are in the Mid West region und missed the
event................. arrrrrrhhhhhh, you missed a great show.

A great event is already being planned for next year but since it's
in the planning stage, i can't tell you what it is. Stay
tuned...........................
