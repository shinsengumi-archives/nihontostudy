Ken -

The styles of Aizu Shoami and Mito are very similar IMHO.
Although I think the better, high end Mito work is a bit
superior. Both schools did a lot of copying of other
schools (Nara especially) work. Both schools produced
tons of shiriimono, but also some nice pieces. I like
the ones you've shown and hope you are able to get them.
IMHO the school doesn't matter as long as you like them.
(Collect what you like, but like what you collect :-)
