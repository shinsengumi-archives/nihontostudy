The Nihon To-ken Hozon Kai (NTHK) is pleased to announce

THE NTHK 2015 CHICAGO Shinsa

April 24th, 25th and 26th 2015 - Hyatt Regency Woodfield - Schaumberg, Illinois

Yoshikawa Eiichi Sensei and the NTHK are pleased to have been invited to Chicago with their team of sword experts to provide sword collectors in the U.S. the opportunity to have their precious swords and sword fittings examined and authenticated under the same strict conditions practiced in Japan.

As this event coincides with the annual Chicago sword show (www.chicagoswordshow.com) held at the same venue, attendance is expected to be high, therefore early registration is encouraged.

Reservations for items to be submitted are $100 each - certifications for authenticated items will be charged an additional $150 - Certificates are the same as those issued in Japan and are sent from the Tokyo office after the event

Time-slots are awarded on a first-come first-served basis so send for your reservations now

Register online at;  www.toryu-mon.com Look for the NTHK registration pages

Stop by our table at the 2014 NCJSC To-ken Taikai in San Francisco or please email us at; nthk2015@...

New service!!

For those who cannot attend the organizers of this years show will handle mail in items
