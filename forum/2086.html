by dougblain

Personally, I think it is a Chinese replica. A much better effort than most of the junk coming from China, but it just doesn't look right. The nakago is very poorly done, the habaki is off, the boshi is mishaped and I think it is brightly polished just to keep it a mystery. Plus it violates the primary rule for buying sword stuff from Ebay, which is NEVER buy anything from China.....

Re: [nihontostudy] Tanto on ebay

by lshobie

HI there, I agree with Doug, it is fake chinese crap. The first give away is that it is being sold from china, the other give aways include the tang, signature, shape of the blade and kissaki, fake fittings etc.

Since you are new to collecting, I suggest going to Rich Stein, and Rich Turners web sites and looking under the "fake sword" sections, they are every informative and will save you alot of money.

Please buy from a reputable dealer or someone you know. If buying from ebay check feedback and stay away from any auctions out of china, 99.99 percent of them are junk and outright liars.

Good luck,

Louis

Note the reference to China? This is not a real Japanese sword. Never consider buying any supposed Japanese sword offered on Ebay from a dealer from China, who is not Roger Robertshaw. And private sale auctions and anonymous dealers are sure signs of fake swords.
