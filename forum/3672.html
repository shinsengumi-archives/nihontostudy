ukiyo-e Japanese war prints

by sworddad

Hi all -

Today's St. Louis Post-Dispatch has an interesting article featuring a local collector of Japanese "war prints" -- woodblock prints -- from the wars with China (1894) and Russia (1904). Not a lot of detail but an interesting write-up neverthless. Thought some of you might enjoy reading it. http://tinyurl.com/8acwl

Obviously this collector and his wife are upper-crust St.Louisans and probably not too accessible, but it sounds like one day they hope to exhibit their collection which would be nice. Again, just passing it along. I'm sure there are a number of list members who collect such war prints. I've always found these interesting but have never allowed myself "another interest." Have enough!!

Ron
