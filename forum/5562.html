Major sale on books - Bridge of Dreams, LLC

by musashibobenkei

In speaking with several good friends and fellow collectors recently, one
thing seems to make sense in times like these, and has been mentioned to me
over and over: it's a great time to hunker down with your books and add to
your knowledge to be able to take advantage of the inevitable buying
opportunities to come.

I've got an inventory of books that's both wide and deep, and have been
grateful for the support of the collecting communities over the past 5 years
- selling books is my only occupation. I came to the conclusion a couple of
years ago that I can only do a good job selling books at the Chicago show -
coming up in just a month - and I've put all my efforts into that show and
my website.

All these books do no good sitting in my stock, so I've decided to offer an
unprecedented 33% off sale through April 18th. Yes, in many cases, perhaps
most, that means I'll be selling for less than my cost, but I need the space
and money, and you need the books.

Check my website, www.bridgeofdreams.com, and check back. I printed a
listing today of all the sword, sword fitting, and armor books I have which
aren't online, and it's hundreds. All those books are lined up or already in
boxes to haul to the Chicago show, and I'll be adding them to the website
daily, but ask if you are looking for something not listed, or want some
recommendations on particular subjects.

You can order on the website using Visa/MasterCard/Amex/Discover or PayPal.
Your card or account is never charged until I confirm the total including
shipping, and I make a point of charging for shipping at my cost. I prefer
to ship Priority Mail, but can use other carriers if you prefer. Email me or
call if you have questions.

Thanks for looking, and I'm hoping to be of service!

Craig Harris
