Nihontopedia

by apedraza_93

All,

I've been mulling over the idea of creating a wiki (open source, user
driven encyclopedia) on Nihonto for a few months now. The idea was
based on my seeing a need for a more comprehensive and
monitored/moderated source of Nihonto information on the world wide
web that would be easily accessible to anyone seeking or having
information on our favorite subject.

Please browse to http://www.nihontopedia.org/ or
http://www.nihontopedia.com/ and take a look. You will need to create
a user id before it will let you publish anything. At this point in
time, I am the only sysop/admin. As you and the rest of the
enthusiasts post articles, this will surely overwhelm me, especially
since I am by no means an expert yet. Let me know if you're
interested in sharing the sysop role with me and I'll consider all
respondents on a case by case basis.

I'm footing the bill for this, both from an administrative as well as
a server/bandwidth perspective, so please bear with me while I work
out some of the kinks, technically and operationally. Also, please
send me your thoughts, ideas, concerns and anything else related to
this little project of mine.

The wiki is 100% empty now, I was going to write a few articles but
it's the end of the week and I only just now finished setting it up
to where I think it's stable. Which reminds me, any bugs you notice,
let me know.

If you like the idea, and I know there are a lot of extremely
knowledgeable nihonto experts here, please start posting some
articles. Also, please send the url out to your contacts not on the
two lists I'm posting this to, Nihonto and NihontoStudy on Yahoo Groups.

Thanks for reading, and I hope you like it!

Best Regards,

Andres Pedraza
