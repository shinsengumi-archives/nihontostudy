One wonders if any Satsuma-to are featured in the Satsuma Koshirae
catalog or if we find Mito tsuba on the swords of Omura Kaboku. I think
what happened was that when a lord did Sankin-kotai and he liked to
show off, he would outfit his guys in matching koshirae. Adding some
new innovation or regional specialty would add kick. Done right these
styles would become popular with the residents of Edo and then spread
to other provinces. Thus we see Higo style stuff from other places, at
the same time Higo enjoyed a thriving business...
