BTW,
I asked Hagihara-sensei whether he had ever seen anything with a Mito Shoami signature or any record of an attribution to Mito Shoami. He said no, but of course that he had seen signed Aizu Shoami work.

We didn't get any of the Shoami-style shiiremono tsuba at this shinsa, but in past shinsa they were simply rejected as being mass produced without any comment as to their origin.
