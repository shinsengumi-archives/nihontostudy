Listers and Lurkers,

A while back a newbie posted a question about
'Swashtika' mon tsuba and whether his wife's purchase
for him was a hong kong crapper. He couldn't provide a
photo, so we couldn't confirm- though evidence pointed
towards it. Several of us had seen this design used in
brass hong kongers recently.

Just revisiting the topic openly, -becuase I can't
find the newbie's email-, here are 2 things that
popped up and seem to me quite legit.

Tsuba from Benson (dead ringer for the design newbie
drew)
http://www.bushidojapaneseswords.com/4montsuba.htm

It has happened that modern kodogu slips through
Benson (I speak from personal experience, I doubt Bob
knew and the items were very good. They are the only
moderns I own.). This tsuba looks legit to me, and
most of what he sells is very good to excellent.
Anyone care to deconstruct this tsuba as not legit?

the Kyo and Shoami as Pete mentioned. I think though that if this was
