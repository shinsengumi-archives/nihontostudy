Re: [nihontostudy] questions about my tanto

by retalt

Hi Sue, nice to hear from you. This is a Naval dagger, a dress dagger
that was worn by Japanese Naval Officers. They sometime came with
genuine tanto in them, perhaps a family heirloom the office wanted to
carry into battle, but mostly they came with a mass produce blade which
was for the most part practical as a weapon, but not genuine Nihonto
(traditionally made Japanese sword).

Tell the group where you are (state/city) and perhaps someone close by
can have a look for you ?

A quick look here will give you a little more info
http://home.earthlink.net/~steinrl/dirks.htm
<http://home.earthlink.net/%7Esteinrl/dirks.htm>

Hope that helps just a little

cheers

Richard
