RE: [nihontostudy] Yari tip

by robsongord

On jumonji-yari made for the Hoso-in school school of fencing, they are
required. Less common on straight yari, and should not be seen on straight
Koto yari. They are fairly common on Shinto ryo-shinogi (four sided) yari.



Gordon
