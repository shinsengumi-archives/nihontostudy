Re: [nihontostudy] school

by retalt

Hi Jon, that is Choshu, not Soshu, the image is a tad small but I can
see Nagato Kuni saku on there. It also note it is late Edo. In Haynes is
states

Choshu Hagi Ju, Nagato province, D: ca 1800. Some say this is the same
artist as Bushu Ju Masasada. He signed Bushu Ju, Kofu Ju and Buko Ju,
Wakayama says he is the same man as Choshu Ju. Akiyama Kyusaku in the
TOKEN KAI-SHI part 97, Oct. 1908 says this name is unrecorded, at that
time, and he did good work.

There are mei references in the Church Collection and Toso Tosogu Meiji
Taikan book 3 page 240

Cheers

Rich
