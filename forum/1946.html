by soshuju

Jim et al-
Don't give up hope yet. There were several "Gen-Xers" who were regular
attendees at the monthly kantei-kai. Most notably the young Mr
Fujishiro and his friends (there were always three or four), they
always dressed sharp and took the whole thing very seriously. While
others used the Dokuhon, they used only the Fujishiro Jiten and often
left early having sussed out the swords with their first bids. There
are young polishers and collectors out there, several young ladies who
study at the Sanno, and others who I am I am sure we never run into.
Who will be the next leaders? Can't say for sure but they are there...
-t
