Rich,
Kofun period tsuba come up at auction from time to time. The Compton and Lundgren sales come to mind. I have two. There was a small iron hoju guard for sale at a show in the states a few years ago. Of course you can see plenty of them in Japanese museums and books on the period.

The corrosion pattern on that tsuba isn't consistent with the pieces I've seen. The rim is deeply eroded, but the other edges and the surface are in great shape with no distortion. Not sure if Tom was joking, but I wouldn't be surprised if he's right.
