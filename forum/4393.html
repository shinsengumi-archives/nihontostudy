by cehlers1949

I just wanted to say Hi and that I am glad I found this group. My father died not long ago
and left two samurai swords which are now in my possession. He acquired them in Papua
New Guinea after the battle of Buna. One is in a plain brown storage case and the other is in
a pebbled case with a leather cover. I hope, over time, to discover all that I can about them.
As my father never talked about his 4 years of fighting in the Pacific theatre, these are the
only link that my siblings and I have to his time there. We hope that as we learn more about
these swords, we will also learn about those times and the valorous men who fought on both
sides. I won't have expertise to share, but look forward to learning a great deal from all of
you. Is it permissible to post photos on this site?

Chris
