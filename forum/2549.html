Fredrik-
Not sure you'll be able to read the kanji, but Shinsenshi is more like
an art "title." For example say the man's given name is Gorouemon his
art name (working name ) is Hirotaka and he calls himself "new son of
the purple sage Hirotaka." Shinsenshi is a name/title he has given
himself perhaps in lieu of the official titles like Izumi no Kami that
we often see...
