identifying utori technique

by sworddad

Another topic which we discussed awhile ago was to understand and recognize the use of gold utori overlay on fittings, what is true "utori" and what is not.

Jim Gilbert mentioned recently that he plans to be at the Tampa show this year if all goes well. I don't recall just who all became involved in our on-line discussion of "utori" but I for one, plan on bringing along a few examples of what "I" call utori and also a few examples of what I have my doubts about being "true" utori technique. Keep this in mind and if we can gather a few of us together for a short while that weekend, maybe with Jim's leadership we could all learn something about "utori" work. I will have a table to sit down at or if not there, certainly a quiet corner can be found somewhere there. I for one look forward to some discussion on this topic!

Ron
STL
