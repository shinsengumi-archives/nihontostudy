How about;
"The blade with a Senjuin Origami" Subtext: your opinion may vary.

At least once at the Kamakura kantei-kai we were presented with a blade
which carried an origami by Hon'Ami Koson, but had since been reevaluated
by
the Kyokai. Sorry I can't remember the smiths off-hand but we were rewarded
with points for pegging the Kyokais attribution or Koson's though they were
different artists. Since the majority of bids were for the smith assigned
by
Koson, the comment was made that at least in this case his attribution was
spot on, but of course the home office is never wrong. Therefore we had to
choose for ourselves which we thought was correct...
