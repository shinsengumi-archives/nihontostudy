Tosogu.com

by retalt

http://tosogu.blogspot.com/

All, I have started a blog, not so much a forum but more a repository
for information on tosogu. We have a few contributors but most are busy
at present and articles a slow coming in, but they will come. I have an
interesting article on Owari tsuba being translated at the moment, and
hope to have several stories on Japan after my October trip.

Please have a look if you have time, this is not run as a forum with
moderators, rules and open discussion, but as a collective wanting to
place as much information on Tosogu in one spot as possible.

If anyone would like to offer an article, just let me know.
