Hi Arnold,

Yes, I am trying to figure out how to buy it,m and I was able to
contact Steve. I had seen the blade before it was polished and also
OKed it for Yushu-saku. It is a very fine piece and I hope I can figure
out a way to put the money together. The Sano trip was, as always, a
real pleasure. Watanabe sensei got me to do some translation work for
her, and she put out some very fine pieces. I particularly like the
Chu-Aoe blade. If you remember Okada san's Yosazaemon Sukesada katana
we brought over last year, the Aoe blade looked just like it -- only
better. It was pretty clear seeing that blade that Yosazaemon was
copying the Chu-Aoe style of workmanship down to the shape of the
blade. Anyway, there will always be a next time.

Gordon
