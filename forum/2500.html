Hi there ( nice to share a name with us ) ?

Good scans, the sword is out of polish and as such, will always be hard
to identify form pictures. The rust on the nakago ( tang ) looks to be
new and given the look of the yasurime ( file marks ) and the shape and
condition of the nakago, my immediate thought was Gendai. Looking at the
first image, with the long Chu kissaki and the low shinogi, I think this
is even more possible. If not gendai, then very late Edo or possibly
Meiji so maybe not Shinto, Shinshinto or later ( i.e Gendai )

Just for some general information, what you call a blade piece is in
fact a Habaki, the spacers are known as Seppa, the handle is a Tsuka.

Just my thought's here.
