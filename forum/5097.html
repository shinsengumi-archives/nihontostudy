ROKUSHO BOOK

by xe83fan

Dear Nihontophiles,

Just doing my daily trawling for all things sword related when I stumbled across this link to the book "ROKUSHO":
http://www.kanshoan.com/special/dtls_book.html

This Japanese website is asking 2,940 yen - on today's exchange rate that's AUD$27.67 or USD$23.81. Of course postage is on top of that and I have no idea what that would be, however, I paid AUD$60 for it from a local collector who no longer wanted it and I was quite glad to have it. I cannot find it on Amazon, but Wikipedia has some information on Rokusho:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rokusho

I found this altogether fascinating and, for a primarily blade man, very interesting indeed. Some of the Wikipedia links under the listed catogories of Artistic techniques | Decorative art | Metallurgy may be of interest to many of you. As John Yumoto once said, there is no end to study...

Best regards,
Barry Thomas.
