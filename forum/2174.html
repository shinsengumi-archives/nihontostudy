Well now I have seen everything. Just got done
watching a Japanese sword take 7 hits from a Browning
M-2 .50 cal machine gun firing FMJ (Full Metal Jacket)
rounds. I have to give the blade its dues it took 7
hits prior to snapping off. This from the same show
that fired at a sword with what I believe was a .44
Magnum could be wrong it was a while back. Just wanted
to let everyone know to keep an eye out for the clip.
Interesting to see the blade slice through the rounds
although the copper jacket did a number on the blade
taking a chunk of the Ha with each hit until finally
the blade just failed.
