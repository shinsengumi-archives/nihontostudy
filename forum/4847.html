Elliott,

Regarding "The Tsuba" (Murtha), you will find that it was one of two publications that followed "THE SAMURAI SWORD: An American Perspective" (1980) before publishing his "Volume II: SWORD FITTINGS" (1984).

Apart from completing a set (four titles in all) you would be able to omit both "The Tsuba" and "JAPANESE SWORDGUARD ARTISTS" (1982) if you already have the 1984 text.
