New to Group - looking for information on this book - as where to buy ? =
Shumi No Sukashi Tsuba ? Kogatama
by
Naruki Kazuhiko, Nakata Kanehide, etc.
This books is quite interesting book showing the way to make Sukashi tsuba,
Kozuka, and the how to polish it. Many professional experts wrote each
chapters and Tokuno Kazuo and the famous 2MY Gendaito smith, Nakata Kanehide joined
to write chapters for Kozuka making. There are many photos and Kozuka price
list and Tsuba makers' value are listed well. Book is in excellent condition.
15.5cm x 21.5cm, Printed in 1980, 166 pages / Like to get a copy in English
?- or any information on Making Tsuba and Kozuka making etc. Thank You,

Conely,
It's "Shumi no Sukashi Tsuba Kogatana tsukuri" published by Kogei Shuppansha, Tokyo. As far as I know there is no English language edition or translation available. <> The price on the dust jacket of my copy is Y2,000. Penciled inside the back cover is "25," so I'm guessing that I bought it in the US for $25. Maybe the book people on the list can track down a copy, although the price will probably be higher these days.
