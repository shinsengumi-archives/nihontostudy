Hello everybody. I'm a newbiy here so I thought I might introduce
myself a little. My name is Leroy and I live in Northern California.

think I am ready to purchase on, and now I would like some opinions.

I was wondering what you would choose. I found 3 swords that I like
and are about in my price range; One was made in 1977, one in the
1930's (papered-NBTHK) and another in the 1860's (papered-NBTHK).

the 1860's does have some light rust, the others are in full polish.
The dealers are reputable.

Any help would be much appreciated. Thanks in advance.
