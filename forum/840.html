fuchi/kashira set mei

by sworddad

Hi all -

While describing a fuchi/kashira set today, one I do not look at very often, my first thought was that it was early work, possibly Momoyama era. Then I realized that it was signed which made me wonder (and my question to you all) if you find Momoyama era fittings very often signed? Well, I guess you do but if so, I have seen very few that were signed. This led me to wonder if this set was really that early or what. Looking in Haynes' books I do not see a Muneyoshi (kao) referenced--without kao, yes, but none with kao. Could read Soyoshi, sokichi...maybe something totally different? Hopefully the links will work (in Yahoo pictures) and if so, I would appreciate any comments that you may have as to when these were made and what era. (apologies for the poor photo...mei is fine, though.)

http://tinyurl.com/p40k

http://tinyurl.com/p40v

Any help or impressions or comments...all appreciated.
