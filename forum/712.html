Hello, All:

I came across an interesting tsuba:
http://www.nihontophile.com/DragTsu1.jpg
http://www.nihontophile.com/DragTsu2.jpg
Condition, work seem to place it mid (or most probably) late Edo.
Very slight seppa zuri indicate it was mounted up.
Tsuba is 3 7/8" tall x 3 3/4" wide. Were "big" tsuba popular in late
Edo? (I thought not..) I wonder what size of blade this would have
been mounted on?..definitely not a "dainty" piece...
