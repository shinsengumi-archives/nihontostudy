Unknown seal ...

by drjohnplissenden

Can anyone out there please identify the seal shown in my photo
album JPL?

It is on an otherwise uninscribed tsuba of openwork iron, depicting
Chinan and the dragon within a slightly raised rim. The carving is
of a very high quality, as is the skillful gold inlay.

The presence of the hira-zogan gold seal, together with the image of
Chinan stirring up a storm is suggestive of Tetsugendo work, but I
believe that the brightness and sophistication of the image together
with the polished, brown surface of the metal exclude such a
provenance.

I personally favour Okawa Teikan (Sadamoto) as the artist, but have
been unable to find this particular seal among the many that this
artist used.

I am sorry about the poor quality of my first effort at posting
photographs, and will try again if they are felt to be inadequate.

Regards, John L.
