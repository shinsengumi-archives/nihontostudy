John,
Shunjo/Mitsunaga was the nidai of the Hachirobei line and that is what the inscription reads, but it is hopelessly fake. Kyo Goto no Kenkyu (1988) by Kasahara Koju and Akimoto Shigeo is the present definitive reference on waki Goto.

Jim

Re: [nihontostudy] Re: Goto F&K, mumei?

by yamaimojg

The workmanship is lower middle level, but not terrible. My guess is that the signature was not done by the maker. The usual forged signature approximates the genuine article. The signature here is totally off in the arrangement of the characters, the placement of the strokes and the broad, chippy style of carving. The maker of this piece could have carved a better gimei.
