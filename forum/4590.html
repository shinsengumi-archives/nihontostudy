Re: Kaishaku under the rain

by excelsiorilluminati

Hi Guys, I just followed this thread on both Yahoo forums and found
it interesting and humourous. There was a lot of testing done at the
execution grounds and it seems probable that a sword of particularly
heavy weight and special construction would be used to test ones
abilities in a one handed stroke. I would hate to be the prisoner,
as, the one handed grip has a tendency to turn in the cut. Most
executions being done by low ranking bushi. I do not think that it
would be used because of the rain though. There would be plenty of
attendants to assist with the restraints, guarding, official
recorders and their attendants that someone could hold an umbrella or
construct cover. The thought of the executioner calling "Bring the
special umbrella holding one handed in the rain sword" is what
brought a smile to my face. John
