Ikkyudo - Owari Tsuba

by ccikkyudo

Paul,
Thank you.

Many folks incorrectly limit their understanding
of Owari tsuba to Owari Sukashi tsuba.
In fact, Owari tsuba-ko comprises a great deal
of non sukashi work staring with Nobuie,
Yamakichibei, Hoan, Sadahiro etc.
Best book on the subject is Owari to Mikawa no Tsuba-Ko.

Also check the Art and the Sword, Vol. 3 (1990) pp. 2-53.
A Comprehensive Survey of Bishu Kinko by Wakayama Komatsu.
(Prepared for the 14th NBTHK Zenkoku Taikai (annual convention,10/83)).
Pages 7-8 discusses Fukui Jizaemon. Plate 25 displays a
tsuba by him which has strong affinities to mine.
(Mine is better IMHO).
