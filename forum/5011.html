Christie's May 16th London Auction

by neobliviscar

Christie's Japanese auction is May 16th. In the
auction are several remarkable Toku Juyo that are
worth a good look. I've seen the Choji in person and
wish I could have seen the Norishige.

Christie's:
http://www.christies.com/LotFinder/lfsearch/SearchResults.aspx?intSaleID=20862

http://tinyurl.com/2cceb9


Also in the auction are several tsuba that belonged to
me previously and are being sold by the current owner.
When the catalog arrived, I was surprised to see
that several of the decriptions were in error and also
made no mention of their NBTHK papers. The errors have
been corrected online, though I do not think all of
the ones with papers have been updated. Among them are
signed and papered Ichijosai Hirotoshi (NBTHK),
Ichiryu Tomoyoshi (NBTHK), and Isso (NBTHK)tsuba. The
shakudo Tomoyoshi was a favorite I regret needing to
sell.
