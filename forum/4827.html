Pete, Barry was pretty well there. From Haynes and Torigoye

Uchikaeshi-mimi: The edge of the tsuba rolled and beaten onto the plate
surface.

Kohineriu-mimi: A type of Uchikaeshi-mimi that has a slight twist to the
rolled over edge.

So I am thinking Kohinerikaeshi-mimi is the same of Kohineriu-mimi.

Hope that helps
