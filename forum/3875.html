Kantei Tsuba #2 - my bid

by retalt

Hi Jim, excellent, and that was fast :-)

ok, here is my bid for Kantei #2

ONO Sukashi

The first thing that struck me was the very bold design, the heavy set
sukashi work. Next, the squareness of the sukashi cut, much like Akasaka
work where the walls of the Sukashi are very straight. As for the iron
itself, it has a somewhat soft look to it, and the thickness is typical
of this group as is the Maru Gata. I have found no reference to tekkotsu
in this group yet so I cannot make a judgement on that either way. All
these things lead me to my judgement of ONO.

When I first looked at this tsuba, both Ono and Yagyu popped into my
mind. One thing I found when looking into this group is that they based
there work style on both Yagyu and Kanayama styles.

Kind regards

Rich Turner
