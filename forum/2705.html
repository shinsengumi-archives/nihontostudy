Hi,
Just a suggestion!!
The second tsuba with "Shukado Wire" is similar to Shoami Shigenobus
work judging by the openwork clouds. Look at my tsuba. Could this be the
same artist or school?
http://hem.bredband.net/odrike/Tsuba2.htm

Also look at Richard Stein’s page he’s got a par of daisho tsuba made by
Shoami Shigenobu
http://www.geocities.com/alchemyst/tsuba/tsubadai.htm

But its only the clouds that are simular dont know if Shigenobu worked
with Shukado.
Regards,
Fredrik
