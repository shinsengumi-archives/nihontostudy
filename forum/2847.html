Re: [nihontostudy] help with tsuba Signiture

by retalt

Dirk, on your scanner, try propping up one end of the tsuba off the
glass, bu about 1/4 to 1/2 inch, then scan the mei area. The angle helps
reflect light of the chisel strokes and can make the mei easier to read.
Try lifting both ends fo rdifferent results.

Rich

Izumi no Kami Koike Yoshiro
Naomasa
