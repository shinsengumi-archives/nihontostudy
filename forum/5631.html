by xe83fan

icepick52,

It would be nice to see you sign with your name if you want people to help you!!

You might enjoy Dr Richard Stein's website:
http://home.earthlink.net/~steinrl/nihonto.htm

and more particularly his origami page:
http://home.earthlink.net/~steinrl/origami.htm

Another website that offers translation guides for papers is Danny Massey:
http://www.nihontocraft.com/

His papers translation guide is here:
http://www.nihontocraft.com/japanese_sword_papers.html

Between these two you should be able to work out most of the translation yourself - and learn something in the process... What you may need further help with is any detailed description aside from the "proforma" language in these guides.

Best regards,
Barry Thomas
(Melbourne, Australia)
