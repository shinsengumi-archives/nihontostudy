Re: [nihontostudy] Re: Photos from 1964 Sword Show

by retalt

G'day Dean, so it is save back out as a Jpeg, just using a different
algorithm.

XnView can be found here at http://www.xnview.com/en/index.html

The first link is the download (*XnView (v1.97.7/1.70)*
<http://www.xnview.com/en/xnview.html>)

Then using the second image (row of armour) as an example I just re
saved it with the default Jpeg settings (80%) and the file size went
from 2.8 meg to 480 K. Quite a difference. It is not as effective as
Photoshop but it's not bad, and I am sure the quality difference with be
almost non existent. Fiddling with the settings won't make a huge
difference.

Cheers

Rich
