Re: [nihontostudy] Polish book

by davidzincavage

Socha, Henryk. Elementy broni samurajskiej - Tsuba [Elements of the Arms of the Samurai - The Tsuba], Warsaw: Dom Wydawnictwo Bellona, 2003.



It can be purchased via:



http://www.merlin.com.pl/





Merlin is essentially a Polish-language Amazon. Go to: Books (Ksiazki), then Search (Szukaj), on "tsuba". The only tricky part is typing a US/Canadian address into the Polish spaces provided.





Hey! look at that, they've eben got abn English-language how to page:



http://www.merlin.com.pl/frontend/static/info/first_purchase/how_to_buy_eng.jsp
