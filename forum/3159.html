Re: Sotheby auction

by davidzincavage

The two in parenthesis does mean there are two items: the tsuba and the the tsuba box bearing a hakogaki by Torigoye.

The 4560 pounds + 20 % buyer's premium of 912 = 5472 x 1.752 conversion rate to dollars = 8760 more or less, but probably a little more. But it is a famous piece, one we all recognize from the book.

DZ
