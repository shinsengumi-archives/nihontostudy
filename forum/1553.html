Gordon, Jim and all,
Let me follow up the shinsa'ing tsuka thread. You may recall the
tsuka I discussed on this list last year that has Jomon age projectile
points set in as menuki. The kashira is clearly old and original but simply
plain iron. The fuchi matched - barely, and the wrap is nice and old.
Overall, I see little here that warrants "papering", but I would like to
get a sense of the age and regional origin of the handle. Would the shinsa
team be able and willing to say anything in a case like this?
I realize that this sound for all the world like the problem I
presented to the last NTHK shinsa with the putative "chokuto". Maybe my lot
in life is to be a thorn in the shins side!
