Re: what theme is this?

by klepet33319

Eckhard Kremers show this crossed bar design in his 'Sukashi Tsuba',
pg. 44, as being Jogi -- ruler or angular measure. There is also a mon
which uses parallel bars as rolls of silk. Fun!
Pete Klein
