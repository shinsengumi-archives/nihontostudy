Hi all,



Just wanted to pass this along to those of you coming to Tokyo at the end of
this month for the Dai-Token Ichi. The NTHK will hold its annual big sword
appreciation meeting on October 30th from 1 pm at our regular meeting place
near Shinanomachi. The theme is famous Koto and koshirae. There is a 5,000
yen fee for attendance. Anyone who wants to attend, but does not know how to
get there can meet in front of Shinanomachi station at around one and I will
be happy to guide you. Hope to see many of you soon. Also, as in the past,
if everyone wants to get together for Saturday night dinner and drinks, let
me know and I will get a reservation made.



Gordon
