I have tried a number of ways to clean blades after cutting. Here us a good
one. A soft cloth with one end soaked in a solution of baking powder, the
other dry. After your cut, wipe the blade with the wet end to remove
debris, then the the dry end. I follow with the uchiko and choji oil. The
alkaline solution wil prevent staining and is similiar to the solution used
by polishers.

Sorry for the lack of precision. I had a couple of e-mails for more
details. Actually Arm & Hammer baking soda. About a heaping tablespoon to

slippery.
