Re: [nihontostudy] Tsuba theme

by Robert Gilmore

The thing on the bottom that looks like a drain grate is the sukashi
form of the upper board with attached wood pieces. The wooden pieces
are loosely attached at the top. The whole board is attached to a rope,
which is strung up. Someone or something runs into the rope; the rope
and board jiggle and the clackers make noise. Seems to be very
inefficient, but you see them in the chambara movies a lot.

Bob
