Hi Mike,

The entire inscription is the maker's name. There is no school name included. I am
pretty sure the name is Hidemichi. The other three characters on the right side
begin with Ryû or dragon. The next two I cannot read. I have asked my wife to take
a look. I have check the Kinkô Dictionary and there is a listing for Hidemichi, but it
does not give his pseudonym. There are a couple of other possibilities for the
second character of the name. It looks like Michi, but it could also be Hideyuki
depending on the number of strokes in the character. Terunori is also a possiblilty
with the same characters as Hideyuki, just a different reading. Unfortunately, none
of these artists use the pseudonym shown on your tsuba.

Gordon
