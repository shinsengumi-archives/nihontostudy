Re: What the heck- someone is stealing my website?

by satcho.michael_harris

Rich is right. Clearly someone is taking your content as their own,
and the only other section is a programmer's website design
recommendations.

I find content from my site popping up all over the internet. In
those few instances where I have asked the party to remove, or
otherwise cite satcho.com as the source, the request is often
ignored. Twice I have been told by (well known) sites owners' that a
third party who listed the stolen content did so without their
knowledge. Then in the most regulated of perfect worlds - the eBay
utopia with its guardians of what is fine, good, and proper, the
auction ends well before receiving a dismissive and self-serving,
automated reply from the eBay to my inquiry.

Ken - I enjoy your interesting - and novel - site documenting the
study of the mantis theme in tsuba, and hope that it continues
undeterred.
