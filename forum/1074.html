Re: [nihontostudy] shoami ?

by yamaimojg

The design is the Higo "crane in the nest," but the execution looks like Kyo Shoami. The sukashi in the seppa dai is decorative, it's part of the leaf motif. I've mentioned this feature before when we were discussing decoration in the seppa-dai. You see it in Hayashi and Nishigaki school work. There are several examples of this exact motif in the Higo Kinko Taikan. I can look up the pages if anyone needs it.

But, the Higo examples aren't carved in partial maru-bori like that. I'm not sure what I would make of it otherwise, but the carving of the tree trunk looks like what you see in Kyo-Shoami guards. I suppose it could be someone else combining the Higo motif with more naturalistic carving.
Jim
