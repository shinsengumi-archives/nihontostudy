Darcy,
The first one is Hiroyuki with a kao. There are several makers listed with that name.

The second is Nara Toshi (Naga?) followed by a kao. I'm not seeing the second character on the left very well, but if you're seeing it as the other "toshi," that kanji can also be read Naga and there are a number of makers signing that way including the Nara Toshinaga of sansaku fame.
