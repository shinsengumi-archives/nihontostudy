Most (if not all?) signed Kunitomo tsuba I have seen
have been iron. I think I have only seen two tsuba
(maybe 3?) signed Hazama that were in iron. Nearly all
the Hazama tsuba I have seen have been copper of some
form, including one with very high grade shakudo
inlaid to image open fans.

I have seen disproportionately more tsuba signed
Hazama than signed Kunitomo tsuba, but wouldn't hang
any sort of statistical correlation on it yet.
