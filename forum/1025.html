A lot are even thicker. Early Akasaka guards will usually have a very full, round maru mimi. Owari guards tend to be rather squarish (but there are exceptions). Some guards that otherwise resemble Owari get put into Ko-Shoami if the rim is more rounded. But these have no where near the rounding of early Akasaka work.
