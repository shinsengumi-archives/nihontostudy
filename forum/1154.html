Brinkley available on line

by yamaimojg

I just started checking out this site and found that they have full scans of at least some volumes of Captain Brinkley's 1901 "Oriental Series."

http://tinyurl.com/w67b


I've been looking through volume one and the quality of the scans is quite good. Anyone who has been talking about making books available on line should check it out for the navigation experience. Of course the content is classic, too. Be aware that every page is scanned, even blank ones, so it takes a bit of clicking to get to the beginning of the text... about 19 pages in.

Jim
