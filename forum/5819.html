auction ripoff

by kdw21620

Hi all,

I just wanted to warn anyone bidding on auctions to be careful of total ripoff
shipping policies. Bonhams in England quoted me a price of 141 english pounds
to ship 1 regular tsuba. I got a quote from mailbox etc which is around 100 and
that is still disgusting. I know it costs about $20 usd say around 15 pounds
to ship. I emailed when I got the original quote and said you must be
mistaken. They said that quote is correct, they use an outside vendor but the
item will be delivered to my doorstop. where else would it be delivered to? I
figured at those prices if they have 5-6 customers in NY they could actually
send a person by plane to hand deliver. I am debating what to do, but since I
haven't gotten a response back asking to speak to a person in charge, it looks
like I won't be a customer of theirs ever again. I am a strong believer in
providing customer service but when an action like this occurs, it just shows me
they don't really care. 3rd party or not you as a company are responsible for
the entire experience. If they chose the vendor, it is still part of their
service. I am just amazed at how much of a ripoff is being allowed to go on.
So be warned and be careful, a company I thought was reputable is a place I
don't think is acting in a reputable manner.

Hope all of you have better experiences.
Ken
