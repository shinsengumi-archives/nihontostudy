Re: It is very quiet here

I am still here!

After thumbing thru many books and web sites, I have decided to name
my mysterious Tsuba (the one mounted on my Hiramasa) Echizen Sukashi.
If you go to my album (Elliott's Album) and look at photo's 1 thru 7,
tell me if you agree or what you think.

I first thought Kyo-sukashi but Gordon said "I don't think so." Then
I was leaning towards Akasaka but the Fukurin was telling me "no". So
I threw my hands up and pondered other stuff.

It wasn't until I was 'talking' to Andy Quirt when I ask him and he
suggested Echizen. I hadn't thought of that but I went to searching
and maybe............it is!

What do you think?

Elliott
