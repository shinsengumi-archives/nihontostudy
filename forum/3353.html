A change of direction ...

by drjohnplissenden

Dear members

Surely I am not alone in bemoaning the apparent demise of this
Nihontostudy group? Once a vibrant and educational medium for many
people sharing our interest, it is now a shadow of its former self.
Messages are posted far less frequently, and often remain unanswered.
Those that are answered seem to produce a rash of self-indulgent
repartee between a few, select members which the remainder of us find
unamusing and tedious.

Is there any hope of a change of direction in order to restore this
message board to its early promise?

JPL.
