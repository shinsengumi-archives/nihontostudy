Rich,
The sukashi pieces certainly have Kanayama-esq designs, but in this case I
have to agree with Cary's comment the other day about difficulty (futility?)
of identifying them without seeing the iron in person. I think that 3 or 4
mm would be unusually thin for Kanayama or even Owari work.

It's also very unusual to see such a thin plate on the inlaid piece.
Interesting pieces.
