I am a Japanese living in Japan.

It's Kanazawa, not Kanezawa

Kashu is another name of Kaga.
Kaga is the old name (Edo period) for the region where Kanazawa belongs.
Now, Kanazawa belongs to Ishikawa prefecture.

For Kanetoyo, I do not know the details
but this is not the first time I see his name.
