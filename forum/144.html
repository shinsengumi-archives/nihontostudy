by gaigin2000

Hi Rich;

Very interesting tsuba. I do not think I have ever seen one like it. Not the design or the unusually shaped large kozuka hitsu. Sort of like some of the early Jingo but different.

The shape of the nakago ana is also unusual in that it does not have the shape you would expect to see that would tightly fit a nakago, and I think it would be difficult to fit a seppa without covering the sukashi design.

The look of the iron on the plate seems a little coarser and I do not see any of the large bones in the surface of the plate. Its hard to tell from pictures though. The hoan tsuba I have handled have been abut 1.2 to 1.3mm thick.

Very interesting unusual tsuba.

John
