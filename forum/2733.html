Re: [nihontostudy] Laminations and tekkotsu

by Barry Thomas

Hmmm. Just ruminating. The laminations are I think the blade equivalent of
kizu - ware - and ideally shouldn't be there. "Bones" I have no idea about
although I think they are small lumps of higher carbon content = harder steel,
so they should stand proud of the surface, like currants in a bun. Your third
pic is difficult because it looks to me like an "ordinary" rusted surface where
the high bits are only high because of the corrosion pits leave them standing.
Do they look like different steel??? Thus draineth my pitiful knowledge...

Barry Thomas.
