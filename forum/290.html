by ccikkyudo

Ron,
This mei can be read:
Right side: Torishiro *TanTo Sha ni oite, Yoshida Katsunori saku.
Tan (kitaeru-to forge)To (swords) Sha (workshop).
Made by Yoshida Katsunori at the Torishiro swordmaking workshop.

Left side:
Kiyomizu (can be read Shimizu)
or if it had a stroke over the Mizu character
obliterated by the mekugiana it would be read Kiyonaga.
Kiyomizu Toshio kun no tame ni.
Made for Mr. Toshio Kiyomizu.
