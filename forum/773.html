by yamaimojg

Ron,
Ichinomiya Nagatsune worked in a variety of kinko styles and had a number of followers. I find it difficult to get my arms around the group because of the range of their output. I have seen some menuki and fuchigashira signed by Nagatsune with animal motifs where the eyes were formed with that same raised ring and dot seen on the large buffalo. Kind of wide awake and a little bugged out. I'm not sure if there were other groups that made eyes quite like that. Not an area that I'm strong in, but that's the only thing that stands out for me here. Anyone else have a better grasp of Ichinomiya?
Jim
