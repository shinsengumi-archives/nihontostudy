Tom,
If you ever get an international eBay page that won't load, they are
all linked. So just change .com.au to .com and it will load as a USA page.
So http://search.ebay.com.au/_W0QQsassZnippon_to will work just as
well as http://search.ebay.com/_W0QQsassZnippon_to

I use this if the link loads the page in another language too.
