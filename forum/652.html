What a guy, Ken Mack! It was in 1992 when my wife began her one year struggle with cancer. At her last sword show--probably in Houston I guess--Ken, who was also struggling and somehow surviving his cancer, had a wonderful "pep-talk" with Kathy that truly helped her to continue her treatments. It amazes me to realize Ken survived another eleven years. He shall be missed!
