Curran,

Could your location be Matsushima???

http://www.japan-guide.com/e/e5100.html

I had the place in my mind but couldn't for the life of me remember its name. I found it here, which site may also be of interest to others:

http://www.japan-guide.com/e/e623a.html

As to the poetry connection, try this (google keywords [ matsushima poem ] no quotes):

http://www.cs.rice.edu/~ssiyer/minstrels/poems/1021.html

and this

http://www.jref.com/practical/matsushima.shtml
which has the comment: "The great poet Bashou Matsuo (1644-1694) came here and exclaimed in one of his most famous Haiku poem : "Matsushima, Ah! Matsushima! Matsushima!"."

Regards,

Barry Thomas.
