Gentlemen:

I have a set of koshirae that appears to have been made by a student
of Goto Seijo (5th). The student is not listed in the Kinko Meikan.
(info from the Murtha fittings volume)

Is there any English language resource on the Goto artisans that
would list genealogies, students etc. ?
What is the best Japanese language resource on this kinko school?

by yamaimojg

Ron,
For sideline Goto the best book I'm aware of is "Studies in Kyo Goto" (Kyo
Goto no Kenkyu) by Koju Kasahara and Shigeo Akimoto, 1988. It is in English
and Japanese. The Seijo family chart shows one student of Seijo Masafuku
(the 5th) and his name is Enomoto Kiyotsugu. He later changed his name to
Kiyohisa. There are some other students of other generations listed too.

Jim
