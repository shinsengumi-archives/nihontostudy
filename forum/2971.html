A curious discrepancy

by retalt

or am I reading it wrong ?.

Looking through the Sano Museum Akasaka Tsuba book, I was going through
the mei of the Tadatoki smiths. On page 98 on plate number 2 is a mei by
the 6th Tadatoki that reads Bushu Junin Akasaka Tadatoki and dated to
Tenmei 9.

The very same mei I believe can be found in Wakayama's Toso Kodogu Meiji
Taikei in Book 2 on page 306 only this is being listed as the Sandai
Tadatoki.

Any clues to this ?. I would have thought Wakayama's books be he bible
of fittings information, though a book that specializes in a particular
school should have it's information right as well.
