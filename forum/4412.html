Elliott et al-
You need only send 14,000 yen to the address below. 12,000 for the
year and 2,000 for registration(12,000 per year thereafter. The
easiest way to transmit your money is by international postal money
order in the US dollar equivalent, make it out to 'Nihon Token Hozon
Kai'. Your subscription starts from the month submitted, so if you
put in your sub this month it will be up each September, if you want
it to run January to January (The NTHK fiscal year), you could wait
or include an extra 4,000 yen for two issues and it will end in
December of next year.
Be sure to include your name and address inside the envelope with a
note "For Membership"...
