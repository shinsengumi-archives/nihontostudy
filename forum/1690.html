Is this book useful/reliable? I have seen a few

pick up on that they were all -famous-.

were

print. Sort of a Public Relations photo opp. for a
sword.

If it is not an insightful or reliable reference,
I'd be more inclined to just stick with the Tokubetsu
Juyo Token Zufu(s).

Thank you for any advice on this work,
