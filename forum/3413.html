by robsongord

Hi Milt,

You would have to ask the team about the menuki, but the fuchi-kashira
should be ok. You cannot submit it as a tsuka, but as fuchi-kashira and then
menuki, so two separate papers. As for the period, you should get Edo shoki,
Edo zenki, Edo chuki, Edo koki, Edo maki, Bakumatsu, Meiji and the like.

Gordon
