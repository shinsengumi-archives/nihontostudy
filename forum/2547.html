Boston MFA fittings on line

by yamaimojg

For anyone who doesn't already know, the Boston Museum of Fine Arts has done a nice job of putting their sword fittings collection on line:

http://www.mfa.org/artemis/default.asp

Entering "tsuba" in the search box turns up 1784 entries. Searching on "Saotome" for example turns up 7 entries, but "Saotome tsuba" yields nothing. Kogai, kozuka, etc., each return a substantial number of hits. I haven't had much success searching on koshirae or swords so far. Image size and quality are adequate, but not great. Some of the attributions are off and the collection is what it is, but it's great that the museum has devoted the resources to make this available to us.
