Re: [nihontostudy] Tsuba with copper chips in the iron

by davidzincavage

I would think the sukashi design represents a sickle (kama), having religious significance as symbol of Suwa shrine (Suwa myojin), and from a warrior's viewpoint used to signify "mowing down the enemy."

The tsuba looks Katchushi to me. I'm afraid I think you are mistaking rust for copper.

David Zincavage
