TSUKA MAKI A Hands-on Seminar

Sunday 17 June from 8:00am to 4:00pm on the last day of the “The Arts
and Arms of the Samurai” show at the Holiday Inn in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

This one-day class would teach the basics of tsuka-maki and when
finished with the class participants will have a wrapped tsuka.

You need no previous background in tsuka-maki, but you will need to
supply your own tsuka, same and fittings (fuchi/kashira & menuki).

The charge is $220 for the class and you will be given ito, paper
triangles, an information booklet, awl, screwdriver and knot tying tool.
The colored booklet will have a step-by-step photo how-to section and
background information.

Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis by letter with
workshop fee. If you are interested or know someone that is interested
more information about the seminar is available by email at
jswords@... or on my web site at
http://www.montanairon.com/class.html. You can reach me by phone most
evening (USA - Mountain time) but I will not return your long distance
calls regarding the class so please call during evening hours Mountain time.

If you know of another sword related email list or bulletin board please
post this information.
