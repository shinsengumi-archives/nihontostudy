Members:

Since I have been trying to keep NTHK (NPO) members up to date, and
promised to relate the latest court decision when released, here is the
quick summary...

A suit was filed over the suspension of the NTHK and Token To Rekishi
trade marks by the Patent Office, both of which were originally applied
for and awarded to the NTHK (NPO) (NPO= non-profit organization,
distinct from the other group, which is a family business).

The court decision, in true Japanese fashion, is that since both groups
have original members of the group before it split, and have been active
continuously, it is not in the public interest to allow either group to
have exclusive rights to the NTHK and Token To Rekishi trademarks.

Thus, we will continue to refer to our group as the NTHK (NPO), and
continue to publish our journal, Token To Rekishi.

I would have hoped for a more cut and dried conclusion to this to
eliminate the confusion caused by two groups using the same titles, but
this decision is, in a nutshell, a perfect example of how things work in
Japan....

Thus, there doesn't seem any end, in the near field, to concurrent usage
of the titles.

Anyway, that's it....
