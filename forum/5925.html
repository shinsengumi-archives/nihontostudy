2013 NTHK West Coast Shinsa

by soshuju

Its that time of year folks!

Q: What is a Shinsa?
A: An Examination

Think of it as Antiques Roadshow for Japanese Swords and Sword Fittings. Do you have an old sword around the house that you have always wondered about? Here is your opportunity to get that sword appraised by experts from Japan, Sword Polishers and collectors with years of experience. For a nominal fee you can have your katana appraised and receive a certificate of authenticity. The same certificate issued by the NTHK in Japan.

And this year there is a new feature, we have a mail-in service available if you cannot make it to the San Francisco Sword Show!

Book your tickets now for the 2013 NCJSC San Francisco To-ken Taikai August 2,3, and 4 at the SF Airport Marriott and please visit our webpage for information on registration for the 2013 NTHK West Coast Shinsa.
http://www.toryu-mon.com/Toryu-Mon/N...ouncement.html

Feel free to send us any questions. Hoping to see you all there,
-t

PS for information on the Sword Show visit;
http://ncjsc.org/SF_token_kai.htm
