Milt,
I've been out of town for a while. The color of the base metal and the rubbed gold overlay look like what's found in the so-called Ezo fittings. Most of these are menuki and saya kanemono, some of which are quite old. I can't recall seeing an Ezo kozuka. It'd be an interesting piece to see in person. We had a nice pair of Ezo menuki at the London shinsa that were assigned to the early Muromachi period.
