Tsuba exhibit coming in July...

by sworddad

Hi All -

This might interest those in the area of Topeka, Kansas. I've been in contact with these people regarding the group of tsuba in their collection but I've no idea as to their quality or reliable documentation, but it might be worth checking out next month. The display will be at the
Mulvane Art Museum, Topeka, Kansas
They advised...
---------------
Opening this Summer at our museum will be a large collection of Tsubas (54), Yatate (39), and over 50 Japanese color woodcut prints in one exhibit. The exhibit will open July 5, 2009 and will be on display through January 17, 2010.

Mulvane Art Museum
17th & Jewell, Topeka, KS
785.670.1124
mulvane.info@...
