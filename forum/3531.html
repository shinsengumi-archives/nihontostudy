Re: [nihontostudy] I have been collecting information

by davidzincavage

Haynes 2:847-848:

MASAHARU
H 03892.0

Family: Ito
Name: Masaharo Zenzaemon
Worked: Odawara ju, Sagami Province and Bushu ju
Date: ca. 1750-1800
Notes: considered the second master of the Ito Odawara school branch of the Ito school. Student of Ito Masatane H 04528. Furukawa catalog pl. 19 #107
Sources: Wakayama Takeshi, Toso Kinko Jiten 355 -L -10, 380 -L -4.

Wakayama Takeshi, Toso Kodogy Meiji Yaikei, 1-313.

Ken'ichi Kokubo Shinsen Kinko Meikan, 345, 371.

(kanji omitted, abbreviations expanded)

him, the woeful evening I had last Thursday. To fill you in, I was
attacked by a cat. Now I know, but stop laughing, it's not that funny.

world ( Milt and Pete Klein will know the first ) and it managed to
damage the 3rd metacarpal and ruptured a vein. It was a bad bite. My
hand blew up to the size of a bag of tsuba and is still extremely sore,

with a blade.

I of course have never done anything stupid with a sword, though I did
whack myself in the eye with a large Heianjo tsuba once. Come on, don't
be shy.
