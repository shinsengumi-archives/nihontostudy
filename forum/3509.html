by robsongord

Hi Pete,

The plate with the design and nanako is cut out of the kogai and that
plate is fitted into the top plate of a kozuka. Usually you can see a
change in the nanako or even a line where the kogai plate has been
inserted into a kozuka plate.

Gordon
