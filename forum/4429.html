Satsuma tsuba question

by neobliviscar

List,

I was recently asked what would seem a rather simple
question: "What do you think this tsuba is worth?"

Usually that is a simple question that I'll give my
2cent answer to if I have any degree of fair
confidence in my answer through what I have observed
here or there over the years.

In this instance, it was a very nice iron Satsuma
tsuba with some gold nunome vines and the Shimizu
(sp?) mon on it. Practically straight out of the
Satsuma koshirae book.

The tsuba didn't knock my socks off, but was a good
tsuba a bit afield from what I collect. Interesting
piece.
I realized then that either I have overlooked any
nicer Satsuma tsuba on the market, or maybe they
simply don't come to market that often? Anyone able to
point me at some Satsuma tsuba in Japan or give me a
fair ballpark what sort of value they carry in Japan?
I'd love to see other examples, if anyone has photos.
I haven't much looked for them before, but now am a
little more curious to learn about them.

Curran
