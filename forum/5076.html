by pbleed

Others will be of more help, but your tsuba is signed "HARUTOSHI". It looks
like Soten work, but it is in very sad condition with the entire original
iron surface gone. The fuchi was mass produced Sendai. Supposedly this
work was done by the under-employed samurai of Sendai to augment their
wages. It is Sendai work, but it was handled by a broker in - like - Nagoya
or someplace.
