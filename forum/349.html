Hey guys,

yesterday I discovered that those digital microscopes that were made by
intel are still
available! some company called Digital Blue is now marketing them.

If you haven't seen one of these before, they're a cheap looking
microscope that plugs
into your USB port and go to 200X - they actually work pretty well
though the focus
knob is kind of gnarly at the higher magnifications - they have a pretty
fair depth of
field and the resolution looks fine on screen - you can also pull 'em
off the stand and
use them freehand for all kinds of macro applications. Best of all, you
can get 'em from
amazon for $49.99 - look for digital blue QX3.

Not as good as a top grade microscope with the camera port, but hey, it
didn't cost
3 grand either...

Check it out...
