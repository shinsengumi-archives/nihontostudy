20% Off - Eight Day Anniversary Sale on all books at Bridge of Dreams

by musashibobenkei

In recognition of my fourth year in business full time selling books, and in
gratitude to the support that all of my friends in the collecting
communities have given, I am announcing an 20% off sale through midnight
7/7/07 - eight full days. (It's just past midnight on the East coast, but of
course it's already Friday afternoon in Australia and Japan, Friday morning
in Europe, so to keep it fair let's say the sale ends midnight 7/7/07
wherever you are.) Please do feel free to pass this message on to interested
collectors who may not be on the several email lists (and excuse the
cross-posting.)

No minimum purchase - or maximum. This is your chance to get a great deal on
Soshu Den Meisaku Shu, Tanobe's Meihin Katanaezu Shusei, the complete 6
volume Kanzan Token Koza, complete 10 volume Haynes Auction Catalogs,
Fukushi's Goto and Machibori books, or the Kokuh么 T么ken Zufu.

All shipping will be done at cost as I always have. Postage rates,
especially for overseas shipments have changed drastically since May 14th,
but I will work with you to get your order to you safely and quickly with
the least expense possible.

Regrettably, if you live in Illinois, I have to charge 8.75% sales tax.

As always, what is listed on my website is not all of what I have on hand,
though I will make every effort to keep updating. If there's something
you've been looking for, please do ask as I very likely may have it.

Payment by PayPal, or direct credit card charge to your Visa, MasterCard,
American Express, or Discover Card, and I will accept wire transfers to
accommodate overseas customers.

Finally, I am aware that the hosting service I use has an expired security
certificate, so if you want to use a credit card you can call me, fax me, or
split the information into two or more email messages. Also, there's a bug
in the PayPal ordering button - yes, I'm moving to a new hosting service and
working with a new web person to fix these things. It's probably best to
email me directly in any case as the shipping for each order will be
different.

Thanks in advance for your continued support!

Craig
