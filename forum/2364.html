Tokugawa Daisho

by schrattenholz

Hi all.
I had the opportunity to buy this in the spring 2004 from 2 private
collectors ( former ). I bought the Daisho set first and then the
owner had seen it on the net and then provided the tanto for sale. I
believe that it is great that all 3 pieces are together now. Maybe
that they some day may come back to The Tokugawa Museum ? Have been
looking at many websites and hae besides NihontoAntiques, as I
received very good recommendation about, - thankyou, also seen www.
artswords.com owned by Bill Rannow who have many fine things on his
site, swords as well as fittings. Does anybody know him or have
bought something from him. It looks very good to me?.
Regarding the Tokugawa, I need a little help to translate the
correct mei on the shoto which I translate as Kunifusa.
Unfortunately I can not differ from Hawleys Kun 34 - 39. I have
tried to take some good photos of the mei but have not succeeded yet
and will return for help when I have some good photos.
I learn a lot from all off you so please continue the lessons,
thankyou.
All the best
Allan
