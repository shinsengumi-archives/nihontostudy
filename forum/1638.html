Kaneyama? tsuba

by ron_polansky

All:

I was wondering about the attribution of this tsuba:
http://www.nihontophile.com/Kaneyama.jpg
Tsuba plate has a deep black (not purple) patina. Pics don't really
convey this but I think early.
Looking at the center of the tsuba and the hitsui ana I thought
Kaneyama. Looking at the outer portion of the plate (with the basket
weave), I don't know.
No prominent bones on the rim.
Pretty tsuba once I got off the gold paint that someone had decided
to coat it with.......

Thanks for looking,
Ron
