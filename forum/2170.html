Care of old sukashi tsuba

by steven_88_2000

Greetings everyone,

I am new to the group, and new to the study of nihonto, so
please indulge my newbie questions as best you can! :o) I
wanted to get your experienced opinon concerning the cleaning
of iron sukashi tsuba. What's troubling me is that Sasano-
sensei seems to indicate in his Early Japanese Sword Guards
that not only the rim and plate of an iron tsuba may be cleaned of
their red rust, but even the sukashi openings. Of course, I have
read what Jim Gilbert says on his Tsuba site regarding cleaning,
and indeed Jinsoo Kim advises just the same: do NOT clean the
sukashi openings of iron tsuba! So I am confused. To clean or
not to clean, that is the question! Many thanks for your informed
opinions... :o)

Steve
