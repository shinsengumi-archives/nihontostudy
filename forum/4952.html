Re: [nihontostudy] photos added to album

by xe83fan

Tom,

It's a pretty lousy MUNE- I have another character in mind but haven't looked for it yet. It is well-known that most signatures on kogatana are spurious, and IMHO these are no exception. Elliot, I am sorry but it pains me to say that the characters are crudely chiselled with no finesse whatsoever. A cursory run through my kogatana book does not reveal any examples signed in such a rudimentary manner. I have found a Tadahiro but it is nothing like the piece under consideration. I doubt there is much to learn from these pieces. It always pays to bear in mind one of Albert Yamanaka's immortal sayings "Looking at bad swords ruins your eye". It is no less true for kogatana... But as Tom says, thanks for posting Elliot. As a counter to Albert's advice, one of my truisms has always been "Look at everything"...

Regards,
Barry Thomas.
