Token Kai-Shi, a series of articles by Akiyama, translated by Henri L.
Joly and annotated by Robert Haynes, was published in parts monthly by
the Northern California Japanese Sword Club starting in early 1997 (if
memory serves) and continuing through '00 or '01.

I remember waiting anxiously each month for the next installment ...
I'll have to dig out my copies now! Thanks for the reminder, David!

One can only wonder why they have not published this as a book.
