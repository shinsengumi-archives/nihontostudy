Some political turmoil was going on earlier this year at the NBTHK and my understand is that this caused people to spin their wheels, shall I say. Mr. Tanobe told us in October that all of this has been resolved and things are moving ahead once again. I've no clue if this has delayed shinsa results although I suspect it has. Early in last year several swords sent over did take a longer than usual time to clear shinsa. At that time, according to what Tanobe san told me, they were simply hit with an unusually large number of shinsa submissions. I suspect it has been a combination of such things that my have slowed things down a bit. Hopefully 2007 will see things back into a more normal mode for them.
