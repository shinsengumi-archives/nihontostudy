To All Japanese Sword, Weaponry and History Enthusiasts,

It's now a great time to plan your 2007 vacation - just open and
view the attachment.

Here is a great opportunity to travel to Japan in April 2007 to
view and study legendary Japanese swords and weaponry.

Visit fantastic museums that feature Japanese samurai
swords, armor, matchlock rifles and other weaponry as well as
historic Japanese castles and military museums in places such as
Tokyo, Nagoya, Osaka, Kyoto, Okayama, Hiroshima, Kumamoto
and other cities. Everyone who has joined my tours in the past has
thoroughly enjoyed having the opportunity to go treasure hunting at
flea markets, sword stores, cutlery stores, antique shops, etc. You
will go to places where very few Japanese sword enthusiasts in the USA
have ever been to! At the annual Japan Custom Knife Makers Show,
you will meet skilled knifemakers as well as Japanese bladesmiths who
always attend the event.

Now is the ideal time to make your plans for 2007 to travel to
Japan where you will see and learn so much to enhance your
knowledge of Japanese swordcraft, weaponry and history.

Email me with any questions. Thanks and happy new year!
