BEWARE; NEW EBAY SCAM

by dgarbutt2000

There is a new scam involving Ebay. When you bid on an item and it is
not sold you may receive an e-mail that purports to be from the seller
offering to sell you the item very reasonably and requesting that you pay by
wire transfer to an overseas bank.

THIS IS A SCAM. THE E-MAIL IS NOT FROM THE SELLER. IF YOU SEND THE
MONEY YOU WILL GET NOTHING BUT A LESSON IN FRAUD.

Somehow an overseas con artist is obtaining the e-mails of bidders and them
attaching the pictures and descriptions used in the original auction.
