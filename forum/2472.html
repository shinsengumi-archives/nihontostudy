Milt,

My thoughts on the little one were Umetada school. The
inlay isn't right for Kaga. Kaga tsuba trike me more
as the surface is super-prepared, then the inlay deep
or hard set somehow burnished, then surface prepared
again.
Your tsuba has more the layered felling of an
Umetada school tsuba. Clean, laid on/in, and left so.
I don't think I have the Izzard catalog.
