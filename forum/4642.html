I'd not memorized that Tsune kanji before tonight. It
is less common that some other Tsune kanji used in
fittings maker names. I don't know the second
character, so I fanned through all the Tsune_____
entries (with that variation of Tsune)in Haynes to see
if I could find a match. Sorry, but I couldn't find a
match.

That doesn't mean too much as Haynes didn't get them
all. Also I've also occassionally come across an entry
where he had a Typo (wrong kanji) for a particular
guy.

On the bright side: it is unlikely to be gimei.
Anyone out there able to verify the correct reading?
