Heads up boys and girls


******************* Virus Alert *******************


Please be aware of the latest computer virus which has been named
Beagle.AZ. Bagle.AZ is a new variant of the Bagle worm spreading rapidly
over the Internet. If you have received any email that contains this
virus, delete it immediately. Do not forward to others, and do not open
the attachment. You will be able to identify the virus because it will
arrive in an email with the following attributes:
Subject: will contain either...
· Re:
· Re: Hello
· Re: Thank you!
· Re: Thanks :)
· Re: Hi
Body: will contain either...
· * :)
· * :)
Attachment: will contain either...
· Price
· price
· Joke
If the attachment is executed, it opens two different Internet protocol
ports on your computer that are designed to allow your machine to function
as a relay to send spam email.
