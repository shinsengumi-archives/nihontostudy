Something to look forward to in October

by kdw21620

FYI- a response I got from the Musuem of Fine arts in
Boston

Dear Mr. Wilson,

Thank you for your interest in our collection of
Japanese swords and sword fittings. There is a slight
misunderstanding as it is the museum's collection of
kodogu that has recently been catalogued, not the
swords, but I am pleased to tell you that a
publication of about 150 pieces will be published next
October. At the same time we will have a display from
the catalogue as well as other pieces of Meiji
metalwork that relates to some of our later kodogu.

I hope you will be able to come to Boston to see the
exhibition, it will be on view for about a year and
the catalogue will be sold in the museum shop, and I
imagine on-line as well. Also I'm pleased to tell you
that at the time of publication of all the pieces from
the collection, which have been photographed for this
project, will be available through the museum's
website.
