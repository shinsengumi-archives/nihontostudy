Tsuba...kantei?

by sworddad

Happy 4th everyone!

I've just posted a photo in my Yahoo Photos folder (Ron STL) -- Tsuba 147.22 -- which I'd enjoy some opinions on. It is iron with brass inlay, kiku design, measures 86 x 84 x 4 mm in size. Inlay represents mostly leaves with a few flowers. The inlaywork overlaps onto the edge of the tsuba. Inlay is about 90% intact on face, 50% intact on backside. Notice there are no hitsu-ana present.

http://tinyurl.com/mhf6d

This tsuba seems too elaborately inlaid to be called Kyo-zogan and I'm curious if perhaps it might fall more into Yoshiro work. Comments anyone???

Ron
STL
