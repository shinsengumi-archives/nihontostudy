The book is about the Dutch collection of Nihinto and some koshirae.
It will be a non-profit book.
Mr. Han made more then 450 discriptions from swords in Holland.
(he made 1 mistake in 35 years of collecting, this sword didn't pas
shinsa)

Now he is getting older and he is stopping collecting swords.
The last sword he bought (back) was Fujiwara Sadakiyo from the
Yamato hosho school.
This blade was sold by Sothebys in the 1970's, he didn't bought it
that time.
It was in gunto mounts.
It was sold for 170 Dutch guldends, about 70US$.
10 years later he saw in the Token Bijutsu that it get Juyo.
So, from this time he want to buy it back.
2 years ago it was for sale again.
I was in Japan for the Kobe Taikai and I took it back for him to
Holland.

from swords and some full colour pictures from koshirea.
