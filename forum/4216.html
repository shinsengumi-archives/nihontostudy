Nihonto books, eBay vs Amazon

by apedraza_93

My usual rant regarding book auctions on eBay, with an added twist.

Amazon price including shipping for new book: $28.36 http://tinyurl.com/pumve
eBay final auction price plus $10 S&H: $55.00 http://tinyurl.com/qybaa

I'm blown away by people willing to spend twice what a book costs
just because they're not willing to spend a minute doing a simple
search. I guess their income must be way higher than mine. Also, I
think I'm going to start selling books on eBay and then just ship
them straight from Amazon, pocketing the difference.

The added twist is that I'm sure most of these books on polishing
have resulted in good blades being damaged by people who think it's
as easy as sharpening the steak knives at home. Polishing should be
left to professionals.

Thanks for listening (reading) to my rant.

Best Regards,
