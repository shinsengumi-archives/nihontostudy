This is the original, first edition Japanese printing of the 'Nakamura Kakudaifu Nobuie Tsuba Shu' by Akiyama, Kyusaku, 1926, Tokyo, or, 'Collection of Rubbings of the Nobuie Sword Guards belonging to Nakamura'. The text is of course in Japanese but the rubbings are the primary source for study of the Shodai and Nidai Nobuie tsuba, not the later reproductions. I will be happy to include a transcription of the Haynes notes from his study with Dr. Torigoye as to the generation/etc. for each page where noted in the Lundgren copy. 144 pgs. A most rare book.
