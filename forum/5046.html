Hey Barry, from R E Haynes index of sword fittings.

SHIGEHARU (H08222.0)

Nara, hisname was JUBEI, he worked in Kando in Edo. Died 1775

Seems to have lived to the age of 70. Student of Nara Toshinaga

That's all except for a listing in the Mosel collection. (page 287 Item 920)

I will try and dig up a meifor you tomorrow and email it over mate.
