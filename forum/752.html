It is with deep regret that I must inform you all that Jim Kurrasch
passed
away yesterday afternoon. Jim has been fighting heart disease for quite
some time now and apparently he died in his sleep while taking a nap.
As
many of you know Jim was one of the "old timers" in the sword collecting
world and specialized in Koto pieces. He was always willing to share
his
knowledge with others and was a firm believer that one could never have
too
many reference books. Jim was a special friend and will really be
missed by
those of us who were fortunate to know him and share his enthusiasm for
Nihonto. It is my understanding that there will be a funeral this Friday
but
I do not have the details at this time.

Roger
