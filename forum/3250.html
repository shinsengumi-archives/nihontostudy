Milt,
The plate and the nunome zogan look more like Shoami work. The Awa Shoami did a lot of that kind of busy nunome, but the sukashi is a bit unusual for them. More profusely decorated guards with that kind of nunome are often called Kyo Kenjo. The fukurin might be original.

Kumagai is the correct spelling. Higo style nunome is a bit different.
