Matt,
I have seen a few jade sword guards in circulation, but I thought that their age was doubtful and their quality not so good even if as old as claimed. I don't know much about jade, but your example looks much better than those. The Metropolitan Museum of art in NY has a somewhat similar Chinese sword on display with a polished, undecorated (but very colorful) agate guard. Some of the early Chinese swords found in Japan have similarly shaped metal guards, although without carved decoration.

I have one of these metal guards that a friend gave me years ago. Pictures here:
