Re: [nihontostudy] book identification

by yamaimojg

It's from Tsuba Nyumon by Shibata Mitsuo.

That type of guard is a fairly common subset of Heianjo-zogan. The deeply worked plate with inlay running over the concavities, the style of inlay and the shape of the kogai-ana are the distinctive features. Yoshiro is an unusual attribution.

Jim
