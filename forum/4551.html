Gordon -- Thank you for the hard work!

by klepet33319

I received the NBTHK AB translations of 'Elementary Course in Sword
Furniture and Fittings (128)' on Mito Kinko by Fukushi Shigeo this
morning and I can see where you were coming from in your comments on
the translation. Work well done and much appreciated. I have to laugh
a bit at the title 'Elementary' as it's very in depth and it really
puts into perspective how much there is to learn in this field. Not
being able to read Japanese of course puts so much out of reach for so
many of us that work such as yours is most highly valued. It also puts
into perspective just how much scholarship there is in what men like
Fukushi accomplish. I will be looking forward to your future endeavors.
Pete Klein
PS: To all -- if you are not a member of the NBTHK AB it is well worth
it, IMHO.
