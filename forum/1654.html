Hi Milt,

The danger with waxing a tusba or any antique metal item is trapping
active rust underneath the wax. This will accelerate the corrosion
and only make things worse.

Best not to bother waxing tsuba's.

Re: [nihontostudy] tsuba wax advice needed

by Peter A Bleed

Milt,
I can't and won't comment on the bidding for this stuff. But I think we
should be careful of it. I still have a jar full of wax that Tom
Buttweiler sold me. Since he has been dead for a long time, it must be 20
years old. I never use it any more. At it best, it is a short-term fix that
really does a nice job of spiffing up a rusty iron guard. Tom used to carry
the stuff to gun shows and such and use shine up rusty tsuba so that he
could turn them around quickly. It seems to deal well with red rust and
scale. I have to believe that it builds up over time, tho, so I think it
would not be a good regular treatment.
Peter Bleed
