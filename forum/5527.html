David et all-

鑑賞
Kansho - Appreciation - does not necessarily imply all the ceremony. Some
blades are laid out for kantei and others just for appreciation
(kansho-to). The etiquette for taking out and examining a sword is called
Kansho Saho (鑑賞ì@)
手入と保存
Teire -Care - and conservation (or preservation)
保存法
Hozon-ho - Rules for conservation

Is there a Kansho-shiki (ceremony)? I think Kansho-saho is what you are
looking for...
