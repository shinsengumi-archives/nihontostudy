by soshuju

あけましておめでとうございます！
Greetings for a very Happy New Year 2012 from the Nihon To-Ken Hozon
Kai. We of the NTHK wish for you a prosperous new year and extend our
thanks to all for your support at the 2010 San Francisco Shinsa and
beyond.
As in the past we look forward to your support in this new year, if
you have not already registered for the 2012 Shinsa coming up in Tampa
now is the time to do so. We are very close to assigning the slots for
the weekend. We are happy to send you the registration forms if you're
interested. Information may also be found on the webpages below.
Remember our very own Jim Gilbert will be part of the Kodogu panel and
kodogu registrations are already up over last time.
The very best to you and yours on this holiday weekend,

Thomas C Helm
