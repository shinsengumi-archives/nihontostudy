Ricci,
The NBTHK European branch meetings are held in various locations including Germany and on occasion in Switzerland I believe. Michael Hagenbusch is the president.

The address listed in the front of Solingen Museum show catalog "Selected Fine Japanese Swords from European NBTHK Collections" is:

NBTHK European Branch
Artur - Kutscher - Platz 2
80802 Munchen

Jim
