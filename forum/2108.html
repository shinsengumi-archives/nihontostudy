Re: mon question

by incu903

> Following the URL,
> Can anyone tell me what is the mon 4th from the bottom
> on the left hand side (a circle with a square inside)?

This is the mon of Matsudaira (Ogyu) family of Bungo funai (Now Oita
prefecture).

This mon is called "Maru ni Kuginuki"(Circle and nail puller")

By the way, 5 th from the bottom on the left is also a mon of
Matsudaira (Ogyu), but of Mikawa (Now Aichi prefecture).

As you may know, Matsudairas are relatives of Tokugawas.
